About the Artist

Andrea Potmak is a contemporary visual artist working primarily with raw oils, plaster textures, and mixed media. Born in Eastern Europe and currently working from her studio in Budapest, her landscapes and non-objective forms investigate structural stillness and muted organic tones.

Her process is highly tactile, often layering materials and stripping them back repeatedly over months to unveil raw depths beneath the surface plane. Andrea’s work is held in private collections throughout Europe.
